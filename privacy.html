Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  Drive Dupe Destroyer v14.0

What This App Does

Drive Dupe Destroyer is a browser-based tool that helps you find and remove duplicate images in your Google Drive. It runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any third-party server operated by the app developer.

Google Account Access

This app requests the following Google OAuth scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive — required to list folders, read image thumbnails, and move files to Google Drive Trash.

This scope is the minimum required to perform duplicate detection across arbitrary Drive folders. The app does not access Gmail, Contacts, Calendar, or any Google service other than Drive.

What Data Is Accessed

  • File metadata — file names, sizes, dates, MIME types, folder structure, and MD5 checksums provided by the Drive API.
  • Image thumbnails — low-resolution thumbnails downloaded temporarily to compute perceptual hashes.
  • Nothing else — no file contents are read beyond what is needed to generate a perceptual hash fingerprint.

How Data Is Stored

  • Access tokens are kept in browser memory only (JavaScript variables). They are never written to localStorage, cookies, or any external server. They expire when you close the tab.
  • Perceptual hashes are stored in your browser's IndexedDB to speed up repeat scans. This data never leaves your device.
  • Your OAuth Client ID is stored in your browser's IndexedDB so you don't have to re-enter it each session. It is never sent anywhere except to Google's authentication servers.
  • Scan settings are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. They never leave your device.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. The app communicates only with:

  • accounts.google.com — Google OAuth authentication
  • www.googleapis.com — Google Drive API
  • cdnjs.cloudflare.com — Font Awesome icons (CSS/fonts only)

Data Deletion

All locally stored data (hashes, settings, Client ID) can be cleared at any time by clicking Clear Cache in the app. This deletes all IndexedDB data. You can also clear browser data through your browser's settings.

To revoke the app's access to your Google Drive, visit Google Account Permissions and remove Drive Dupe Destroyer from the list of connected apps.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date at the top will be updated. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
